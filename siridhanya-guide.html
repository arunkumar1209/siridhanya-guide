🍵 Ambali — The Elixir

Fermented millet porridge (Ambali / Khameer) is the most potent form of consuming Siridhanya. Soak any positive millet for 6–8 hours, cook as a thin gruel, cover with a clean cotton/khadi cloth, and ferment for 7–8 hours. This fermentation produces probiotics, corrects gut microbiome imbalance, and is the key to natural Vitamin B12 synthesis. For serious chronic conditions, consuming Ambali for all 3 meals for 6–9 weeks accelerates recovery.
